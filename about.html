About us

The Premier Criminology and Penology Institute

The Institute of Criminology and Penology,(ICP)Nigeria is an educational and professional institute established to promote wide research and expertise in the areas of criminology,penology and their many sub-disciplines across Nigeria;directed at professionalization of Criminology and Penology practice in this part of theworld,to align with globalpractice. Institute of Criminology and Penology is an independent,non-political,non-religiousand non profit making body,governed by a Governing Council ofexperts and professionals in the field ofcriminology,penology,law enforcement,forensic pathology etc.It is approved by the Federal Ministry of Education,Guaranteed by the Federal Ministry of Justice and Incorporated by Corporate Affairs Commission(CAC-RC919290) on 10th November, 2010.

Our Mission

Our Objectives

  • To develop and promote the practice of Criminology and Penology in Nigeria
  • To provide policies, guidelines, and procedures with world standards for the practice of Criminology and Penology in Nigeria.
  • To educate and train prospective individuals on Criminology and Penology practice. Also to accelerate security awareness in line with the New World order, by training and awarding Certificates of Proficiency.
  • To carry out research and make publications on Criminology, Penology, and Security issues for effective criminal justice delivering in Nigeria.
  • To be at the forefront of the attainment of international standards in the practice of criminology and penology in Nigeria.
  • To organize forums for professional criminologists and penologists for the exchange of ideas
  • To organize workshops and seminars for professionals to update their knowledge in line with what is obtainable in other nations in the world.
  • To enter into partnership with other institutes of criminology in the world for exchange programs.
  • To initiate and influence legislations in Nigeria in favor of criminology and penology practice in Nigeria.
  • To provide information and education on crime and punishment-related issues by way of publications.
