OUR ACADEMIC CERTIFICATE PROGRAMMES

Note that the Institute is a distant learning for our affiliated Universities and other higher Institutions that offers academic programmes in Diploma, Advance Diploma, PGD, etc., that are legally accredited and recognized to award academic Certification.

Through our partnerships with affiliated universities and higher institutions, we bring world-class education directly to your doorstep. Whether you're seeking to enhance your professional credentials, advance your career, or delve into the fascinating realms of criminology and penology, our programs offer a pathway to academic excellence and personal growth.
